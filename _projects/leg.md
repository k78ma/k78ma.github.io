With [Thomason Zhou](https://www.linkedin.com/in/thomasonzhou/), [Theo Lemay](https://www.linkedin.com/in/theo-lemay/), [Alyzen Jeraj](https://www.linkedin.com/in/alyzenjeraj/) and [Yonji Lim](https://www.linkedin.com/in/yonji-lim/), we turned two [SO-101](https://github.com/TheRobotStudio/SO-ARM100) robot arms into a pair of legs. This included designing a custom "pelvis" part to connect the legs, a platform to teleoperate them, as well as a Mujoco simulation for RL.
